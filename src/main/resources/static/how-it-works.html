How it Works

  • First, you enter your personal information and the usernames to your social media accounts.

  • With this data, the Twitter API is used to search for keywords, which are compared to those you input.

  • Based on the global average, your threat level is calculated.

  • These findings are reported which determines how secure you are on the internet
